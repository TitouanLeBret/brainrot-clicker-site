Frequently Asked Questions

💸 I made a purchase but didn't receive my item

Go to Settings (⚙️) inside the app → tap "Restore Purchases". This will sync all your past purchases. If it still doesn't work, email us with your Apple ID (not your password) and we'll investigate.

🔄 How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Open the app → Settings → "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy. All non-consumable purchases (Premium Mode, Remove Ads, HD Mode, Glows, Skins) will be restored for free.

🏆 My Game Center score isn't showing up on the leaderboard

Make sure you're signed in to Game Center on your device: Settings → Game Center → Sign In. Your score is submitted automatically when you play. It may take a few minutes to appear.

🎰 I lost my Gacha pulls after reinstalling

Gacha pulls purchased with Sigma Shards (in-game currency) are consumable — they get used when you spin. Purchased pull packs (real money) can be restored via Restore Purchases. Free daily pulls reset every 24h by design.

📱 The app crashed or won't open

Try force-quitting the app and reopening it. If the problem persists, delete and reinstall — your purchases will be restored via Apple's system. Email us if the issue continues.

💡 Your Aura progress is saved locally. A reinstall will reset game progress but NOT your paid purchases.

🔕 How do I turn off notifications?

Go to your iPhone Settings → Notifications → Brainrot Clicker → toggle off "Allow Notifications". You can also manage this inside the app in Settings (⚙️).

🌙 Offline farming doesn't seem to work

Offline Farming must be unlocked via the in-app purchase. Once unlocked, close the app fully and reopen it — your accumulated Aura will be calculated automatically based on time elapsed (capped at 8 hours).

🔁 How do I request a refund?

All refunds are handled directly by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the purchase and request a refund. We have no control over this process.
