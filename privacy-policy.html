Privacy Policy

Brainrot Clicker: Aura Edition  ·  Last updated: March 2026

TL;DR

We collect almost nothing. Your game data stays on your device. We don't sell your data. Ever.

1. Who We Are

Brainrot Clicker: Aura Edition is developed and published by Titouan Lebret ("we", "us", "our"). If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: contact@brainrotclicker.app

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum necessary to operate the game:

  • Game progress — Aura count, upgrades, owned skins, settings. Stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. Never sent to our servers.
  • Game Center data — If you sign in to Game Center, Apple shares your display name and score with the leaderboard. We do not store this data ourselves. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Purchase history — In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple's App Store. We receive confirmation of which items you've purchased, but no payment details.
  • Notifications — If you grant permission, we send daily reminders. No personal data is involved.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • We do not use analytics SDKs (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude)
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties

4. Advertising

The app may display advertisements. If so, these are served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your Apple Advertising Identifier (IDFA) if you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. You can opt out at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on your device.

You can permanently remove all ads via the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase.

5. Children's Privacy

Brainrot Clicker: Aura Edition is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require account creation or any personal information to play.

6. Data Storage & Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Leaderboard scores are stored by Apple through Game Center infrastructure.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete, export, or correct. To reset all game data, use the "Reset Progress" option in the app settings. To manage Game Center data, visit your Apple ID settings.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Apple Game Center — Apple Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob (if ads are active) — Google Privacy Policy

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
